About Dr. Hafsa Hayee

Dr. Hafsa Hayee is the founder of Mindfulpaths and a physician, educator, and integrative well-being guide. Her work brings together medical training, contemplative practice, and trauma-responsive care to help people navigate stress, burnout, and life transitions with grounded presence.

Through Mindfulpaths, Dr. Hayee partners with healthcare systems, universities, and community organizations to co-create spaces where healing-centered leadership can flourish. Her approach amplifies the intersections of neuroscience, compassion science, and practical mindfulness tools, allowing participants to connect with their values while cultivating sustainable habits of care.

Rooted in curiosity and cultural humility, Dr. Hayee advocates for accessible wellness education that honors lived experience. She brings warmth, clarity, and a belief in collective resilience to every circle, course, and consultation she facilitates.

Our Approach

  • Integrative: We weave neuroscience, mindfulness, and cultural humility into immersive learning experiences.
  • Trauma-responsive: Each offering centers safety, choice, and practices that honor the body’s innate wisdom.
  • Collaborative: Programs are co-designed with partners to meet real-world needs and sustain long-term impact.
  • Evidence-guided: Research-backed frameworks inform every curriculum, from clinical burnout prevention to leadership coaching.
